Hostel Rules and Regulations
  • Entry to hostel after 10 PM needs prior permission from the warden or proctor.
  • No cooking is allowed inside hostel rooms.
  • Visitors not allowed beyond hostel main gate.
  • Avoid noise and celebration after 11pm.
  • Students are responsible for the cleanliness of their rooms.
  • If a student wants to go on a leave, it must be approved by the proctor in charge and warden.
  • Kettle and iron not allowed.
  • Mess timings: Breakfast 7:30-9:00, Lunch 12:00-2:00, Dinner 7:00-9:00.
  • no gamblind allowed.
